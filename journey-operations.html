Collaboration Environment

Public Meetings

From 21.4.2024. Team & Community Meetings will start. They will be open 24/7 categorized by topics and responsibilities where humanity can work on a synchronized progress as a unit. Purpose of any meeting can be to process specific theme or general tasks that are open on a global to-do list. This will be usually determined by the sub-communities leadership. After initial set-up of global collaboration system per each human’s interest, all sub-communities will cross-collaborate on bridging process of 2 segments of knowledge. In the common collaboration portal for all sub-communities, there will be periodical meeting with Life Team and Ownership where the Community will hear our instructions, concepts, visualisations, integration through bridging and elaborating on branching and blasting(fusing). At the second part of the each global meeting community in collaboration with Community Admins and Team Admins will present summary of findings and submit final versions of knowledge and content for review from Ownership & Team.

Life Team
  • Meetings management
  • Processing submitted content
  • Human verifications
  • Processes implementation
  • Administrative duties
  • Specialized tasks per each sub-community
Community
  • Attends meetings
  • Submits new or edited content
  • Votes and decides on all provided options
  • Community ideas processing in shared collaborative document with all relevant content
  • Socializing and bonding
  • Non-specialized simple tasks that are easily verifiable by public community, AI, or our Life Team
