Investment Wealth Tax System Categorization


Since we are here to help the ones that need the help the most, we have wealth taxation system in place to protect them:

- If financial resources used for purchasing cryptocurrencies were generated through our life system, there is no wealth taxation for any amount

- If an individual or an organization buys cryptocurrencies with resources that were verified by our “100% sustainable” department, there is no wealth tax charged

- if an individual or an organization does not satisfy the criteria of “100% sustainable”, there will be wealth tax additionally deducted during The Great Recalculation

- The further sources of resources are away from our “100% sustainable” model, wealth tax will be proportionally increased

- Taxation brackets:
  • There is no wealth tax on the investment value under 10,000$
  • Wealth tax for investment values above 100,000$ will be taxed 24%
  • Wealth tax for investment values above 1,000,000$ will be taxed 44%
  • Wealth tax for investment values above 1,000,000,000 will be taxed 4%

- The reason values over 1bn are taxed smallest wealth tax is to use those large amounts for instant growth and creating charity projects
